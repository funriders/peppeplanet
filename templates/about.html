About Peppe Planet

Something More Than Happiness

Peppe Planet is a next-generation Family Entertainment Centre brand that brings together indoor play zones, party spaces, cafe and toy stores under one colourful roof. Our centres provide an engaging environment where children, teens and adults can play, laugh and connect.

About Us

Welcome to Peppe Planet – A Universe of Fun!

Peppe Planet — Kollam’s largest indoor play area — is now open at Dream Mall, Kottiyam! Step into a colourful world filled with exciting play zones, soft play areas, adventure activities, and interactive games for kids of all ages. Designed by India’s leading play experts, Peppe Planet offers a clean, safe, and fun environment perfect for daily play, weekend outings, and family time. From toddlers to pre-teens, there’s nonstop entertainment for everyone. Parents can relax knowing safety and hygiene are always top priority. Peppe Planet is also the perfect spot for birthday celebrations with customizable party packages for a fun, stress-free experience.

Location: Peppe Planet, 3rd Floor, Dreams Mall, Kottiyam

Who We Are

India’s Trusted Indoor Play & Party Experts

Peppe Planet is the flagship indoor FEC brand of Funriders Entertainment Pvt Ltd – part of the Funriders Group, one of India’s only integrated, ISO-certified play equipment manufacturers. We have successfully completed 1200+ indoor and outdoor play projects across India and abroad, designing safe and inspiring spaces for children.

Our vision is to build India’s largest network of gadget-free play spaces that nurture happy, healthy and confident children. Every Peppe Planet, Little Peppe, Peppe Party and Peppe Toys store is crafted with the same passion for quality, safety and fun.

1200+ play installations delivered across India and overseas.

ISO 9001, 14001 & 45001 certified manufacturing and processes.

TÜV-tested equipment and international safety standards.

Gadget-free spaces designed for active, social and imaginative play.
